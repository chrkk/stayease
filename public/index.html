What StayEase does

Everything a landlord needs

Tenant Management

Add and manage boarders, assign rooms or beds, and keep complete tenant records in one unified database.

Billing & Payments

Automate monthly rental fees and shared utility expenses. Record transactions and track payment status effortlessly.

Room & Bed Monitoring

Get a real-time view of bed availability and occupancy status across all your rooms and boarding houses.

Maintenance Logs

Document and track maintenance tasks per room. Monitor pending and completed repairs to keep your property in shape.

Rental Agreements

Manage rental agreements with start and end dates, monthly rates, and status tracking for every tenant.

Violation Tracking

Log and classify house rule violations by severity. Maintain a clear discipline record for every boarder.

About StayEase

Built for Filipino landlords

StayEase is a condominium and boarding house management system designed to replace manual paper-based logs with a centralized digital platform. From billing to maintenance, everything is tracked, organized, and always accessible.
